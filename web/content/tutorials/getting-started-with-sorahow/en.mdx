## 1. Create your workspace

Sign in with your Sorahow account and create a new workspace. Choose the "Knowledge Base" template to pre-install tutorials and resource sections.

## 2. Configure locales

Head to **Settings → Localization** and enable both English and Chinese. Set English as the default locale and ensure "Sync structure" is turned on to keep page slugs aligned.

## 3. Publish your first story

Open the tutorial library and duplicate the "Starter" entry. Update the frontmatter fields, write your content in Markdown or MDX, and click **Publish**.
